About

Connecting a startup to investors and enterprises, Enterprises to Startups

I have a belief that humans should rise up on their own. However, the impact of the environment can not be ignored. Now humanity has entered into the fourth industry, and despite the many creative ideas and technologies, individuals and companies find themselves unable to realize their dreams due to lack of favorable geography, culture, and/or resources.

Over the last fifteen years, my career has been in helping clients to manage their wealth as a Financial Advisor. However, when I thought about our world and its business environment, I decided to choose a path to help develop companies that contribute to the 4th industry, helping humanity.

Through our geographic advantages (Silicon Valley, USA,) our business experience, and our network, we will provide quality consulting services and partnerships to help companies that accept and create new technologies stand out in the global market.

Ultimately, the selected companies will receive support from venture capitalists, business consultants, and/or other companies through our advisory board (consisting of Silicon Valley performers and start-up experienced field engineers).

Currently, our company focuses on IoT, AI, Blockchain, Social Impacting.

Sincerely,
